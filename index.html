NerdWork

NerdWork is a team project, built with Java and PHP, created in the context of the Software Engineering course. Any student that holds an academic email from the Applied Informatics Department can sign up and use Nerdwork in order to keep track of his university courses.

NerdWork excels when it comes to managing appointments between students and professors, helping both sides manage their appointments with ease, without the need of direct and simultaneous communication.

Further information can be found in its GitHub repository.
